Be Ready in Hazard-Prone Areas

Here are tips and knowledge you need to stay safe before, during, and after disasters.

(Narito ang mga kaalaman at tips kung paano maging ligtas bago, habang, at pagkatapos ng sakuna.)

üõ† Disaster Safety Tips

üåã Volcanic Eruption

Before: Prepare an emergency bag, know the evacuation route, wear masks and goggles.

During: Evacuate if told, avoid river channels and low-lying areas.

After: Stay indoors, avoid ashfall, clean roof from heavy ash.

üåä Flood

Before: Elevate valuables, turn off power, monitor weather updates.

During: Move to higher ground, avoid walking in floodwater.

After: Disinfect items, avoid contaminated water.

üå™ Typhoon

Before: Secure roof, stock food, listen to forecasts.

During: Stay indoors, keep emergency radio on.

After: Inspect damage, avoid fallen wires.

üåç Earthquake

Before: Secure heavy furniture, practice drills.

During: Duck, cover, and hold.

After: Evacuate if unsafe, check gas lines.
